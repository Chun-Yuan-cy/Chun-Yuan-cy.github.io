About Me

I am a PhD student at the Utah Graphics Lab, University of Utah, supervised by Prof. Yin Yang. Before joining the University of Utah, I worked as a graphics researcher at Tencent. I received my Master's degree in 2022 from Zhejiang University. My research interests include physics-based simulation, geometry processing, and deep learning.
